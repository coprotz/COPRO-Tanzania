About Us

Established in 2018, Copro Tanzania is the first website in Tanzania which connects all construction stakeholders in one platform for the purpose of sharing information regarding the construction products available in the country and outside.

Right from the start we had a clear mission that remains as strong and clear today: to provide marketers at business brands with the tools, insight and inspiration they need to grow and succeed – as both individuals and businesses.

Copro (Construction Products Online) prouds to serve as the focal point for the construction marketing sector, and a force for greater connectivity, enabling manufactures, suppliers and other related professional team including construction contractors, quantity surveyors, architects, engineers, equipment rental firms, local fundis, valuers, surveyors, construction specialists firms and customers to share experiences and learn from one another.

Copro is dedicated to provide accurately information and established a database of all products manufactured and supplied within the country and make all necessary information available to all stakeholders. Now no need to hasle to find information of any product related to the construction industry, Copro is ONE STOP CENTER for you.

Copro also serve as instrument for the quantity surveyors to get Unit Rate of any items and hence easiest the exercise of build up rate. We also provide tool for any person who wants to get estimation of building materials of their proposed building, The tool(estimator) is freindly use even to the one who doesn't have strong QS techniques though minor skills are needed.
